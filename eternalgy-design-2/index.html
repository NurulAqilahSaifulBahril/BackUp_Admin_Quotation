Do / Don't
✅ Do
Use weight 800 for all monetary values
Use green only for positive outcomes
Step labels: uppercase, gray
Keep cards white on muted green page bg
Use #0d1f0f for hero / featured info
Use #22c55e (light green) on dark bg only
Pill tags: border-radius 20px
❌ Don't
Use light weights (300/400) for numbers
Use green decoratively without meaning
Use step labels as section headings
Add drop shadows to cards
Overuse the dark card style
Use light green on white (fails contrast)
Use square or rectangular badges
